VIDEOGRAPHY

Edit raw horror film footage

The goal of this project was to compile 40 minutes of raw footage from a horror short film that was supplied to me and create a polished finished product using Adobe Premiere Pro. Although I did not film this, I had to review the footage, pick the best takes from each clip, choose the order in which the clips go together, cut between different camera angles and back and forth between scenes, add basic transitions, add stock audio and suspenseful music that matches to enhance the listening experience and adjust all the audio levels to ensure everything blends together smoothly, while remaining intense.

Interview with Nursing Professor

The goal of this project was to create an emotional interview that feels personal. Our interviewee is someone who is a nursing professor and teaches nursing. They speak about the current state of nursing and how nurses are not fully prepared for reality once they graduate. All visuals, location-scouting, pre-planning, lighting, b-roll, editing, audio, script and directing the interviewee were completed by my partner and I.
