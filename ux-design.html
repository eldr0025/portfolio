LORENZO’S Kanata Website Redesign

The goal of this project was to do a full website re-brand for our teacher who acted as a client to simulate a real-world client interaction environment. Throughout this project, my team and I developed various deliverables like mood boards, personas, logos, a creative brief, sitemaps, and a branding guide which ultimately led to the theme, layout, imagery, typography, color scheme, and the overall design of these high-fidelity wireframes.
